    Introduction

    I am an Assistant Professor (ATER) at the University of Pau and Pays de l'Adour in France. I completed my Ph.D. through a cotutelle agreement between the University of Pau and Pays de l'Adour (LIUPPA Laboratory) in France and the University of the Basque Country (HiTZ Center - Ixa Research Group) in Spain.

    My research spans multiple disciplines, with a current focus on multilingual social media analysis. This includes Information Extraction (IE) and Natural Language Processing (NLP), particularly using deep learning techniques to collect, process, analyze, and enhance multidimensional data (e.g., spatio-temporal, semantic, etc.). I am also engaged in Data Visualization projects that are essential for developing decision support systems and for effectively communicating complex analyses to non-specialists in fields like tourism, transportation, and public policy.

    I have teaching experience across various levels, including Bachelor 1, 2, and 3, as well as BUT 1 and 2, covering topics in Computer Science, Economics, and Insurance. In 2023, I coordinated the Web Development course at the University of Pau and Pays de l'Adour.

    Introduction

    I am a third-year Ph.D. student enrolled in a joint Ph.D. program (cotutelle) between the University of Pau and Pays de l'Adour in France and the University of the Basque Country in Spain, with my defense scheduled for September 2024.

    My research is diverse and interdisciplinary. Presently, I am focused on multilingual social media analysis, which encompasses Information Extraction (IE) and Natural Language Processing (NLP), especially through deep learning-based approaches with the objective of collecting, processing, analyzing, and valorizing multidimensional trajectories (e.g., spatio-temporal, semantic, etc.). Additionally, I am actively involved in Data Visualization projects that are crucial for the development of decision support systems and for presenting complex analyses to non-computer scientists in various application domains (e.g., tourism, transportation, public policies).

    I have experience teaching at various levels, including Bachelor 1, 2, and 3, as well as BUT 1 and 2, across different fields such as Computer Science, Economics, and Insurance. In 2023, I coordinated the Web Development course at the University of Pau and Pays de l'Adour.

    I am currently seeking opportunities for postdoctoral fellowships to further my research and academic career.
